Dr Kabiruddin Khan (P. I.)

Dr Khan, is an adjunct assistant professor (MSCA Fellow) for chemistry at the University of Gdansk (Poland). His expertise lies in developing computational methods/models for designing safer chemicals (i.e., pharmaceuticals, personnel care products, endocrine disruptor chemicals, organic chemicals, nanomaterials, ionic liquids, etc.) that are safe for human health and the environment.

See Publications »

Dr hab. Agnieszka Gajewicz-Skretna (Mentor)

Dr Gajewicz-Skretna, female, is an adjunct assistant professor for chemistry at the University of Gdansk (Poland). She has a large experience in developing machine learning models in the context of studying relationships between the structural features of NPs and their physicochemical properties and other adverse effects of interest.
